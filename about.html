Our Mission

Financial clarity for every Indian

We believe that financial freedom begins with financial clarity. Most Indians have wealth spread across dozens of places — bank accounts, gold lockers, PPF, EPF, real estate, mutual funds — with no single view of where they stand.

WealthMap changes that. We give you a clean, real-time dashboard of your complete net worth so you can make confident decisions, track progress, and build wealth deliberately.

We're not a bank. We don't sell investments. We have no commissions or hidden incentives. We're just a tool — designed to be the clearest financial mirror you've ever looked into.

How It Started

A spreadsheet that grew into something more

WealthMap started as a personal spreadsheet — a simple file to track assets and liabilities across bank accounts, gold, PPF, and home loan. Every month it would get updated manually, formulas would break, and the version history became a mess.

The frustration was the same question every time: "What is my actual net worth right now?" Not last month. Not approximately. Right now, including everything.

"I wanted something that looked as serious as the money I was trying to track — not another basic spreadsheet or a cluttered app built for the US market."

So WealthMap was built — a clean, India-first, real-time wealth tracker with beautiful visualisations, Firebase-backed security, and categories that actually match how Indians hold wealth. What started as one person's problem is now used by thousands of Indian families.
