Finance Tips

Simple rules for
building wealth

01
Track before you invest
You can't improve what you don't measure. Map every asset and liability first.
02
The 50-30-20 Rule
50% needs, 30% wants, 20% straight to savings and investments every month.
03
Clear high-interest debt first
Credit card debt at 36% p.a. destroys wealth faster than any investment can create it.
04
6-month emergency fund
Park 6 months of expenses in a liquid fund before aggressive investing.
